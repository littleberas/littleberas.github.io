Little Beras Playgroup

Little Beras Playgroup and coffee morning is held in the Bulfin Estate Residents Association Hall (BERA Hall), Connolly Avenue, Inchicore, Dublin 8 from 10am-12pm on Thursday and Saturday.

Due to high demand, there are no ‘walk-ins’. You need to be a member of the WhatsApp group to book your place. Places are made available on a weekly basis. To be a member, please fill in our contact form!

There is a small fee of €2 per family. This covers tea/coffee and biscuits and running costs.

We have some car parking which can be accessed from Connolly Avenue, or the hall can be reached by Luas from the Goldenbridge luas stop.

We welcome local families with babies and toddlers from 0-4 year old and their parents/guardians or carers for a relaxed friendly meet-up.

This is not a drop-off service, children must be supervised by their accompanying adult at all times.

For safety reasons we operate a limit on the number of people in attendance. If you are interested in attending, please join us via our registration form.

For more information see our group guidelines or email us at hello@littleberas.com
