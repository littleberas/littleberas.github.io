Join Us

You need to be a member of our WhatsApp group to get notifications about available spaces. Sign-up below to join the WhatsApp group.

You will also need to have the Google Docs app on your phone to add your name to our weekly sign-up sheet.

We check new registrations once a week. This means it may be several days before you are added to the WhatsApp group.

If you have any questions or would like to get in touch you can email us at hello@littleberas.com
